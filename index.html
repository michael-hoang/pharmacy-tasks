Email Refill Call Goals

Send an email to the refill team, copying PV2 and supervisors, providing the refill call goals for the day. Additionally, include any remaining refill calls from previous days and remind them to review the Refill Review queue and resolve any pending requests in WAM.

Update Shipping Dashboard

Count the DCS and FedEx shipments for the upcoming 5 business days from the WAM Shipping queue (custom queue), and proceed to update the numbers in the Excel spreadsheet available at the following link:

Delivery Shipments.xlsx

Send a reminder in the Refill Team group chat on Teams to distribute the shipments evenly across the week instead of concentrating too much on any particular day.

Receive Drop Ship

In the morning, FedEx drop ship packages are directly delivered to the Outpatient Pharmacy, while UPS drop ship packages are typically delivered to the Inpatient Pharmacy. UPS packages usually consist of drugs sourced from Amerisourcebergen's alternative distribution center, directly from the manufacturer (such as Prevymis, Tukysa, and manufacturer replacements), and some third-party vendor drugs (like Aubagio).

If there are any missing packages, check with the Inpatient Pharmacy store room staff and the dock. If the packages are not found, contact the infusion pharmacy and IDS pharmacy to see if they have received our missing packages.
